Collaborative Research: Integrating Fluorspar Ages and Geophysical Models to Constrain the Timing and Mechanisms of the Collapse of the Cordillera in Southwest North America.

This project website is for NSF Award #2317868: "Collaborative Research: Integrating Fluorspar Ages and Geophysical Models to Constrain the Timing and Mechanisms of the Collapse of the Cordillera in Southwest North America.", funded by NSF-GEO FRES Program

Our Mission

The proposed research work aims to provide direct constraints to the timing of the volcanism in the Western US since Cenozoic via the geochemical dating of fluospar.
